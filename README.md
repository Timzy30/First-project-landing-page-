# Tiger Tech Landing Page

This is my first personal front-end project: a responsive business landing page built using HTML and CSS. The project focuses on clean layout, clear structure, and effective presentation of services with strong call-to-action sections.

The page is designed to introduce a brand, highlight services, explain value, and direct users to contact via WhatsApp.

---

## 🚀 Sections Included

- **Hero Section** – Introduces what the landing page is about and what I do, with a call-to-action button linked to WhatsApp.  
- **Services Section** – Highlights what I build: modern designs, clear layouts, and device-friendly websites.  
- **About Section** – Explains why users should work with me and how I help improve business appearance, productivity, and online presence.  
- **CTA Section** – Encourages users to take action with a “Message me on WhatsApp” button.  
- **Footer** – Copyright notice and brand name (Tiger Tech).
